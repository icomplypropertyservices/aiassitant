What is Ai Business Agent?

Purpose of this application

The purpose of Ai Business Agent is to provide businesses with an AI agent operations platform. Operators use Ai Business Agent to staff their company with AI agents, train those agents, connect productivity tools, and measure usage — under one login at aibusinessagent.xyz.

What Ai Business Agent does

  • AI agent console — create companies and projects; run a Main AI Orchestrator and specialist agents.
  • Training — upload files and programs so agents work with your business knowledge.
  • Integrations — optionally connect Gmail and other apps so agents can act with your permission.
  • Google Sign-In — authenticate your Ai Business Agent account; connect Google APIs only when you choose Connect.
  • Marketplace (AgentBay) — browse and hire agents and skills.
  • Billing — monthly token meter; pay by card (Stripe) or crypto.
