About Ai Business Agent

We built Ai Business Agent for operators who run real companies.

Purpose: Ai Business Agent is an AI operations console. It helps businesses organise companies and projects, staff them with AI agents, train those agents, connect tools (including optional Gmail), and manage usage with a transparent token meter and marketplace (AgentBay).

Most AI tools give you a chat box. Teams still need companies, projects, permissions, training materials, and a bill they can understand.

Ai Business Agent is a multi-tenant workspace: subscribers organise work as Company → Projects → Tasks, staff it with AI agents, and train those agents with the right files.

The product app lives at aibusinessagent.xyz/agents. This marketing home page is aibusinessagent.xyz.
