Privacy Policy

AI Business Assistant · Last updated: 15 July 2026

This policy describes how AI Business Assistant (“the App”, “we”) handles information when you use our website, API, and mobile applications.

1. Who we are

The App is operated by the organisation that published it on the App Store / web (iComply Property Services / project owner). Contact us via the support channel listed on the App Store listing or your account dashboard.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data — email, name, password hash, company/project names you create.
  • Content you provide — agent configurations, chat messages, tasks, templates, uploaded settings.
  • Usage & billing — plan, token meter, credit balance, and related transaction metadata (payments processed by Stripe when used).
  • API keys you store — optional third-party keys (e.g. AI providers) encrypted on our servers; we do not use them except to fulfil your requests.
  • Device permissions — microphone / speech only when you use voice features, processed on-device where the OS allows.
  • Technical logs — IP, user agent, error logs for security and reliability.

3. How we use data

  • Provide authentication, workspaces, agents, and AI chat.
  • Meter usage, enforce plans, and process subscriptions/top-ups.
  • Improve reliability, prevent abuse, and comply with law.

We do not sell personal data to data brokers.

4. AI processing

Chat and agent actions may be sent to configured AI providers (for example Anthropic, xAI, or self-hosted models) solely to generate responses for you. Do not submit secrets or regulated personal data you are not allowed to process.

5. Storage & security

Data is stored on our hosting providers (e.g. Vercel for compute, managed Postgres when configured). Transport uses HTTPS/TLS. Access is protected by authentication tokens.

6. Retention

We retain account and content data while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward for backups, dispute resolution, and legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support (subject to legal holds).

7. Your rights

Depending on your location (e.g. UK GDPR / EU GDPR / CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export personal data, and to object to certain processing. Contact support to exercise these rights.

8. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect their data.

9. International transfers

Servers may be located outside your country. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

10. Changes

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date will change. Continued use after changes means you accept the revised policy.

11. Contact

For privacy requests, use the support email on your App Store listing or account settings.
